We need your help in our effort to overcome the evil of bureaucrcy! Please share this message and preorder your enrollment to this amazing service.

Keep track of the requirements to apply for grad school, have someone pick-up and translate the official trascripts from your school and help you filling out forms to apply for student visas. FAQ

How much will this service cost?
Finding out about the required red tape of thousands of procedures will be free of charge. Enjoying the help of our Burogurus will cost an annual rate based on the amount of help you'll be needing from them.
What does preordering mean?
Preordering helps us become a viable service and it grants you access to the first year of Burogurus at a rate that is lower than the standard rate once the service is launched.
